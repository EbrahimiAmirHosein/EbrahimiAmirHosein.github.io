My name is Amirhosein Ebrahimi, I have a bachelor's degree in computer engineering from Shahid Behehshti University. I am a researcher at Institute For Research In Fundamental Sciences - IPM since 2019. Generally, My primary interest involves computer vision, machine learning, deep learning, cognitive science, and hardware acceleration of machine learning algorithms. More speciﬁcally, I have a great deal of enthusiasm for generative models like GANs and Diﬀusion models and their computer vision applications that are helpful for ﬁelds like medical image analysis, generating high-resolution images for better analyzing and detecting anomalies or diseases in pathology, object detection in self-driving cars and surveillance cameras. Also, I am very excited about machine learning optimization and developing and modifying algorithms with more promising outputs and convergence. Anomaly detection, zero-shot learning(ML) and one-shot learning(CV), federated learning, and adversarial robustness are topics that grabbed my attention. I am Also highly interested in accelerating bio-inspired neural network. I have been preoccupied with a sea of questions regarding How humans make a decision and how we can mimic its mechanism in machines, and I am highly interested in research in this area.
